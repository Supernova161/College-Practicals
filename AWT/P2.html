Poster Presentation: The Importance of Bees in Ecosystems

Introduction
Bees play a crucial role in pollinating plants and maintaining biodiversity in ecosystems.
Benefits of Bees Challenges
1. Pollination of crops 1. Colony collapse disorder
2. Biodiversity preservation 2. Pesticide exposure
3. Honey production 3. Habitat loss
Research Findings
Our research shows that bees are declining due to a combination of factors including habitat loss and pesticide exposure.
Conclusion References
